Privacy Laws

What is DMN's privacy policy?

DMN is a Dutch-based company that strictly adheres to GDPR. DMN's motto is to be a company that acts as the bridge between the respondent and the company that wrote the survey. Therefore DMN does not directly trade your personal information.

Can DMN see the responder's personal information?

By default, DMN does not see the responder's personal information. However, the personal information of the respondent is essentially divided between the part that is provided to the company and the part that is provided selectively, and the company that provided the survey within the range agreed by the respondent will be able to view the personal information.

How much personal information does DMN store?

Your personal data will be stored for 3 years, after which you will be notified of your personal information every year, and you can ask for removal of your data at this time.

Is the response of my survey guaranteed to be anonymous?

This depends on the choice of the company providing the survey, and respondents in the survey can choose at the beginning of the survey about anonymity.
